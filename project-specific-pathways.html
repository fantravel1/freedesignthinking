Why Project-Specific Pathways Matter

Designing a mobile app requires different considerations than designing a community service. Each project type has unique user contexts, constraints, and success metrics. Our project-specific pathways ensure you're using the right brainstorming techniques and prototyping methods for your specific challenge.

Whether you're a beginner or advanced practitioner, these pathways provide the structure you need while leaving room for the creativity that makes design thinking so powerful.
